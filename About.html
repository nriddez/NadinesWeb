About me


I'm a fullstack developer with a sharp eye for frontend and user-friendly design. My journey into tech is grounded in a deep curiosity for problem-solving, creating, and building solutions that make a real difference. Before stepping into the tech world, I spent several years working in the humanitarian field — and that passion for human rights and social impact still drives me today.

I’ve also worked closely with children and individuals with special needs, both as a camp leader and in supportive roles. These experiences have shaped my sense of empathy, patience, and resilience — qualities I carry with me into every project and team I’m part of.

In my free time, I love exploring new places, hiking in nature, training hard (hello HIIT and CrossFit!), and getting creative. I’m curious by nature, always eager to learn, and happiest when I’m surrounded by people, and collaborating with others on meaningful ideas.
